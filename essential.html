Must visit places in the town

Traditional cuisine and famous historical monuments

Featuring a dash of Mediterranean influence, as well as a significant contribution from the Ottoman cuisine, the Bulgarian dishes are known to be very delicious and at the same time affortable. These are some of the traditional dishes that could be found in almost every restaurant:

  • Banitsa - layered filo pastry with cheese

  • Tarator - creamy cold soup made from yogurt and cucumbers

  • Kiselo Mlyako - meant to be one of the best types of yogurt due to the 2 probiotic types of bacteria in it

  • Moussaka - similar to Greek moussaka with the difference that instead of using egg plant it's potatoes.

  • Shopska salad - salad made with cucumbers,tomatoes,onions,peppers and grated white cheese
